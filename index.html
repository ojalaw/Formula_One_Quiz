Rules

- The quiz is made of of three different difficulty levels: Rookie (easy), Seasoned Driver (Medium) and Expert (hard).

- There are 10 questions in each difficulty level.

- Users will only get one attempt at each question.

- If the user has the correct answer they will be notified and the answer will turn green.

- If the user has the incorrect answer they will be notified and the answer will turn red.

- 1 point is scored for each correct answer for easy questions, 5 for medium and 10 for hard.

- If the user gets the answer incorrect, the correct answer will not be displayed!
